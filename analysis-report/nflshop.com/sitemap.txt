## URL Pattern Analysis

### Team Page Pattern:
/{team-name}/t-{team-id}+z-{zone-params}

### Category Page Pattern:
/{category}/d-{dept-id}+z-{zone-params}

### Team Category Pattern:
/{team-name}/{category}/t-{team-id}+d-{dept-id}+z-{zone-params}

### Player Page Pattern:
/{player-name}/a-{athlete-id}+z-{zone-params}
/{team-name}/{player-name}/t-{team-id}+a-{athlete-id}+z-{zone-params}

### Product Page Pattern:
/{team-name}/{product-slug}/t-{team-id}+p-{product-id}+z-{zone-params}

### Brand Page Pattern:
/{brand-name}/br-{brand-id}+z-{zone-params}

### Special Collection Pattern:
/c-{collection-id}
/{team-name}/{collection-name}/t-{team-id}+c-{collection-id}+z-{zone-params}
